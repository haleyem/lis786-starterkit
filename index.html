Welcome!

This is a collection of mostly online resources that can be accessed for free, without any library card or univeristy enrollment requirements. The goal of this resource collection is to provide a balanced and less-biased overview of feminist concepts, with a focus on US American history and politics. It is not comprehensive, and is not meant to be. It is a starting point.

{{page.feminism}}

How to Use This Guide

This guide includes foundational knowledge to help the learner understand feminist ideas, theory, and politics.

  • The basics page provides term defintions and background information you will find used across feminist thought.
  • The philosophy page contains information on specific feminist philosophies, or ideologies, such as Marxist feminism.
  • The topic page sorts all posts on the site by topic, such as feminist movements, LGBTQ focused feminism, and terminology.
  • The webpages & articles page links to external resources, such as articles in newspapers and journals, or feminist blogs.
  • The journals page contains links to open access journals.
  • The video & audio page contains links to YouTube pages and videos as well as podcasts with feminist content.
